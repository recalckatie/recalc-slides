Slide 12 — What Makes an AI Personal Assistant Work

The Building Blocks

What Makes an AI Personal Assistant Work?

Memory

What It Is
Provide context about you, your work and how you like things done
Claude Example
Have Claude take notes on your conversations in a notes folder.

Skills

What It Is
Teach how you want things done, similar to a checklist for a new employee
Claude Example
Ask Claude to create skills in real-time as you are executing tasks

Connectors

What It Is
Connect the AI to your systems (email, calendar, internal drive, team chat etc)
Claude Example
Use Rube, a connector service, which makes connecting tools surprisingly easy
Memory, skills, and connectors are very powerful ideas that enable the AI personal assistant, but are remarkably easy to do in practice

📅
Scheduling

Inbox Triage
Scan recent emails for anything outstanding. Draft messages in your tone with relevant context of what you're working on
